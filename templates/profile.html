About Tadawy

The pharmacy profession is a strong and vital part of the health care system in any country, and a strong pharmaceutical industry is often the key to enjoying better health care. Out of this conviction that we believe in, we established, with God’s help and grace, our first pharmacy in 2021. We started with one branch, but our ambitious vision helped us excel and pushed us to the position we enjoy today. The role of pharmacy goes beyond just providing medicine, we strive to provide an unprecedented range of comprehensive services to help our clients lead healthier and more beautiful lives.
